A little about myself

Hello! I'm Gloria, thanks for stopping by.

I'm a Technical Project Manager based in San Francisco Bay Area. I'm currently a Dev Bootcamp student and an Adobe Digital Academy scholar. When I'm not making tech at my desk, I'm a full-time mom and wife.

My interest lie where functionality meets beautiful design. Living abroad for several years, away from family and friends, I have a passion for making the world a better connected place.

I'm originally from the North of Mexico and I have traveled to several countries in past few years, but California now, holds a very special place in my heart. I try to take advantage of everything that the Bay Area has to offer. On a weekend day, you are likely to find me and my family, eating Asian food, running or biking along the Bay Trail, kayaking in Foster City's lagoon or exploring a kid-friendly location in San Francisco.
